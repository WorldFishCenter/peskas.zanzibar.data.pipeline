# Preprocess General Survey Information

Processes general survey information including trip details, fisher
counts, and survey metadata from WorldFish survey data.

## Usage

``` r
preprocess_general(data = NULL)
```

## Arguments

- data:

  A data frame containing raw survey data

## Value

A data frame with processed general survey information including:
submission_id, dates, location, gear, trip details, fisher counts, etc.
